About Me

She attended Mew Canaan high school till 10th grade where she then transfered to The Academy at Swift River in Cummington Ma. She attended Northwestern Connectict Community College from 2009 to 2011 and obtained her associates degree in science. She then went to school for her cna certificate and worked as a cna for four years. She is now pursuing a career as a computer programmer and is attending an online program at Uconn. She currently works in retail and lives in Torrington Ct while she pursues a different career path.
